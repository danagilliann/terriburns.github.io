about
Since moving from California to New York City, I've worked with a number of startups as well as global enterprises like Google in various capacities. I founded Hackers of Los Angeles and (a few years back) Telluric Season Magazine. This January I helped build an Interactive map for the nonprofit Children International along with 21 other bright women engineers at Square's biannual Code Camp. I check the S&P 500 from Beyonce Trader, which I helped build with my teammates on the executive board of Tech@NYU, NYC's largest student-led tech organization. With Tech@NYU, I lead our Startup Week initiative, our flagship event and incredible weeklong series of technology-related events. The cool startups I've most recently worked for include LB Tech, Chicks Can Code, and the Early Adopter Network. I'm currently a Student Consultant with the NYU Google Apps Department. I also really like Drake, gel manicures, and increasing diversity and representation in the technology industry. I'm slowly but surely teaching myself web development through this ever-evolving website that I've built from scratch.
